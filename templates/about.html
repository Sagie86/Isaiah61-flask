What You Need to know About Isaiah 61 Ministry Nigeria

In the year 2013 around November 18( Which is the birthday of our spiritual mother) Isaiah 61 ministry Nigeria was birthed in Aladja town in Udu local government in Delta State. Papa Gary and Mama Jean (our spiritual parents) After much prayers were moved by passion for souls to come to Africa for mission work. They discussed this with their friend/partner (Apostles Isaiah $ Christiana Kadiri). With their support and encouragement, Isaiah 61 ministry started her first branch in Africa at Aladja where Isaac and Debbie became the pastors over the new work . They moved down to Aladja and faced the mission work fully from this time to January 2014 when Our spiritual parents (Papa Gary and Mama Jean) Came to commission the new work and ordained Isaac and Debbie into the Apostolic office to grow, train up and expand the church in Nigeria. Where there is a will, God will always make a way. This is the beginning of a wild passion that drove Isaac and Debbie to opening branches, training hands and sending them to pastor these branches.

Pastor Ransom Robert who was the first worker/pastor that followed us down from Benin to Aladja to support in the mission field was trained up for Kingdom assignment. While he was still in school at Federal polytechnic Idah in Kogi state, he caught the vision for a branch of the ministry to be started up. After praying through. Apostles Isaac and Debbie approved the request for a branch in Idah. Preparations began and to the glory of God we mobilised the brethren for evangelical work and traveled down to Idah(about 6-7hrs from Aladja) for a four day weekend crusade and revival program. Heaven backed us up and we started the branch in Idah where Pastors Ransom and Joy pastor now.

Our burden is to bring into the kingdom about two billion souls for Jesus. All we care about is that the Lord will gain the souls He died for and at the end of it all, the Glory and the credit will be His forever. We are human vessels called and ordained by God to pioneer this vision. We can't do it on our own, we depend solely on the Lord for Grace, wisdom and the needed anointing for this global assignment. One receives the vision but great is the company of people who publish it. If your heart is truly crying for genuine revival for endtime harvest of souls and the liberation of nations from satanic destruction join us today in this revival flight. We are ready to love the Lord and serve Him with all our hearts, resources, talents and special abilities all for His glory and the interest of His kingdom.
